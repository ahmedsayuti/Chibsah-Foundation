Welcome To

CHIBSAH FOUNDATION

At Chibsah Foundation, we are dedicated to transforming lives by providing healthcare, clean water, and educational support to underprivileged communities in Ghana. As a subsidiary of YAC Group, we believe that access to essential resources is a fundamental right, not a privilege.

Know More

Our Mission

Chibsah Foundation seeks to empower underserved communities by providing essential healthcare, clean water, and educational resources, ensuring a brighter and healthier future for all.

We want to create a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to fundamental resources and opportunities for growth and development.

Compassion

We believe in serving with empathy and understanding.

Sustainability

Our initiatives focus on long-term impact and self-sufficiency.

Integrity

We uphold transparency and accountability in all our actions.

Why Us

Through our health initiatives, water and sanitation projects, and educational programs, we empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future. Our work is driven by a deep commitment to sustainability, inclusivity, and positive social impact.

With the support of our partners, donors, and volunteers, Chibsah Foundation continues to expand its reach—ensuring that every person, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to lead a healthier, safer, and more educated life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Chibsah Foundation is a nonprofit organization under the YAC Group, dedicated to improving lives through healthcare, clean water initiatives, and educational programs. We focus on empowering underserved communities by providing essential resources and opportunities for a better future.

You can support us by making a donation through our website, volunteering for our outreach programs, or partnering with us as a sponsor. Every contribution, whether financial or in-kind, helps us create a lasting impact in the communities we serve.

Our initiatives primarily support underprivileged individuals and communities in Ghana, with a special focus on children, families, and vulnerable groups who lack access to healthcare, clean water, and education.
